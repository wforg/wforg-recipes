Ingredients
	2/3 cup butter, softened
	1-2/3 cups sugar
	3 eggs
	2 cups all-purpose flour
	2/3 cup baking cocoa
	1-1/4 teaspoons baking soda
	1 teaspoon salt
	1-1/3 cups milk

Directions
	In a bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. 
	Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt; add to creamed mixture alternately with milk, beating until smooth after each addition. 
	Pour batter into a greased and floured 13-in. x 9-in. pan.
	Bake at 350° for 35-40 minutes or until cake tests done. Cool on a wire rack. When cake is cool, dust with confectioners' sugar or 
	frost with your favorite frosting. Yield: 12-15 servings. If Cooking for Two: Freeze unfrosted cake in serving-size portions.
